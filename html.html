How to Use the Metronome

Basic Controls

  • Click "Start" to begin the metronome
  • Use the number input to set your desired BPM (40-220)
  • The "+" and "-" buttons adjust the number of beats per measure
  • Click "Stop" to halt the metronome

Understanding Subdivisions

  • 1/4 (Quarter Notes): Basic beat, one click per beat
  • 1/8 (Eighth Notes): Two clicks per beat
  • 1/8T (Eighth Triplets): Three clicks per beat
  • 1/16 (Sixteenth Notes): Four clicks per beat
  • 1/16T (Sixteenth Triplets): Six clicks per beat
  • 1/32 (Thirty-Second Notes): Eight clicks per beat
  • 1/32T (Thirty-Second Triplets): Twelve clicks per beat
  • Swing: Eighth notes with swing feel (delayed second eighth note)

Visual Feedback

  • Main beats are indicated by stronger flashes and sounds
  • Subdivision beats have lighter flashes and softer sounds
  • The beat counter shows your current position in the measure
  • Different colors indicate different beats in the measure

Tips

  • Start with slower tempos when practicing complex subdivisions
  • Use visual feedback to help internalize the rhythm
  • Practice switching between subdivisions to build rhythmic flexibility
  • For swing feel, focus on the uneven spacing between beats
